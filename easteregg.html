EASTER

EGG

The Easter Egg in Tapolca is a vibrant public artwork
created by Judit Varjas. The artist painted important
symbols of the town on the egg, including the
Malom Lake, the Lake Cave, and colorful koi fish. This
unique piece beautifully connects local identity with
folk-inspired design.

It has become a cheerful landmark in Tapolca,
especially during the spring season. It invites
visitors to explore the town’s cultural and
natural treasures through art. The artwork
highlights how local symbols and traditions
can come together in a modern public space.

KALOCSAI MOTIF

The Kalocsai motif is a traditional Hungarian folk
symbol characterized by rich floral patterns and
bright colors. It represents joy, renewal, and the
celebration of life. These motifs reflect harmony
with nature and preserve cultural heritage through
decorative art.

DID YOU KNOW?

Many visitors enjoy spotting Tapolca’s
famous symbols on the Easter Egg,
turning it into a small visual treasure hunt.
