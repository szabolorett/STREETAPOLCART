TAPOLCA

"THE HIDDEN GEM CITY"

Tapolca is a charming little gem in the heart of
Hungary, best known for its unique Underground
Lake Cave and the picturesque Mill Pond. Beyond
its natural wonders, the town also embraces
creativity, Tapolca is home to several street
artworks that add color and character to its
historic streets. A perfect blend of nature, culture,
and small-town charm, Tapolca offers visitors an
unforgettable experience.
